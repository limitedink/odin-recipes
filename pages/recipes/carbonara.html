Creamy Carbonara Recipe

Description

This Creamy Carbonara recipe is a plate of heavenly, creamy pasta. Silky spaghetti with crispy guanciale/pancetta/bacon/ham in a super creamy and cheesy sauce. Simply delicious and so easy to make at home.

Ingredients:

  • 500g pasta of your choice
  • 1tbsp olive oil
  • 80g butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 250g bacon
  • 200g mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 300ml pure heavy cream

Steps:

  1. Cook pasta in a large saucepan of salted boiling water according to packet instructions. Drain well.
  2. Meanwhile, heat a large frypan over high heat. Add oil, butter, onion, bacon and mushrooms. Cook, stirring for 5 minutes or until bacon is crisp and mushroom is tender.
  3. Add cream. Bring to the boil. Simmer 2 minutes or until slightly thickened. Add pasta and cheese to pan. Stir through sauce until heated through. Cut heat stir yolks through sauce. Season with salt and pepper.
